Hot Dogs

Description

A simple and delicious recipe

Ingridients

  • Sausage 1 or 2 for person
  • 2 Onions
  • Hot dog bread
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayo
  • chopped springles chips

Instructios

  1. In a cooking pot, boil the sausage for 7 minutes
  2. Chop the onion
  3. Crash the springles chips
  4. Took the hot dog bread and put a sausage
  5. Put the rest of the ingridients
