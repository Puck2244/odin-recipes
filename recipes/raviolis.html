Raviolis

Description

Raviolis with a bolognese salse, a simple and wonderful recipe, for two

Ingridients

  • 500 gr of raviolis
  • 500 gr of tomatoes
  • 500 gr of ground meat
  • Peper as you prefer
  • Salt as you like
  • Oregano, as you like
  • Rosemary just a few
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • 1 onion
  • A cup of red wine
  • Butter
  • 300 gr of parmesan cheese

Instructions

In a big cooking pot

  1. Cook the rosemary in buter for a few minutes
  2. Add the chopped onion with the garlic
  3. Add the 500 gr of ground meat
  4. Boil the raviolis for 15 minutes
  5. Add salt and peper
  6. Add oregano and the cup of red wind
  7. When everything is done serve with cheese.
